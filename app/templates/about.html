What is Journal-Rank?

Journal-Rank is a place for opinion based reviews of academic journals by readers/authors. There are many popular metrics for ranking journals like citation/ publication count etc., but to my knowledge there is no place for individuals to submit opinion based reviews so I made Journal-Rank to address that.
